Stacks and queues are abstract data structures that have to abide by certain rules 
Stacks have to abide by LIFO (last in first out) push and pop operations on a stack should be constant time
Queues have to abide by FIFO (first in first out)

Big O of queues and stacks is always
insertion - O(1);
removal - O(1)
searching - O(N)
access - O(N)
